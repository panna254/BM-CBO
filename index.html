Building Resilient Minds, One Youth at a Time

Welcome to Bold Minds CBO, a youth-led community initiative dedicated to advancing mental wellness among young people in Siaya County and beyond. Our mission is simple but powerful: to create safe, inclusive spaces where young minds are empowered, heard, and supported.

    Our Story

    Bold Minds was born out of a collective desire by youth in Siaya to challenge the silence around mental health. Seeing friends and peers silently battle depression, addiction, trauma, and loneliness, we came together to provide a bold, compassionate response. We're here to let young people know: You are not alone.

    Our mission is to promote youth mental health through accessible support, awareness, and community-led solutions tailored for the realities of rural and urban Kenya. Whether you're seeking help, looking to give back, or want to learn more—you're in the right place.
